Livestock Monitor

Continuous welfare monitoring across the herd: animal presence and per-individual respiratory waveforms via channel state inference (CSI), body temperature, activity, feed and water levels, barn environment (temperature / humidity / NH3 / CO2), and escape detection. 48 animals across 4 species streamed from an ESP32-S3 sensor mesh.
